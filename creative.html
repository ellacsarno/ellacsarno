Writing

Adjacent to my work in communication, I also worked as a journalist and writer, with pieces focusing on technology, society, and art. I was an intern, later junior staff writer at Index.hu, Hungary’s leading online news site, and I did freelance work for various Hungarian and international publications.

Work examples:

  • Telex.hu – “Do you feel as if the news are biased? You’re not alone.” – Pop science article on the hostile media effect and the 2023 Israel–Hamas war. Published: Nov 18, 2023.
  • Taipei Times – “Successful Turnout at Taipei Dangdai.” – Report on the 2nd edition of Taipei Dangdai, organized by the team of Art Basel Hong Kong. Published: Jan 21, 2020.
  • World of Young Cinema (Dazoo) – “In the cinema, I am, ideally, not alone” – A Portrait of Maike Mia Höhne, the curator of Berlinale Shorts. Published: Feb 16, 2018.
  • Index.hu – “Who needs child pornography when you’ve got Youtube?” Investigative article that uncovered deep web forums where Youtube aggregator software code was circulated for easy access to video content made by children. Published: July 31, 2017.
  • Index.hu – “A Tinder date for $100.” Investigative piece on the relationship between the then-new Tinder app and middle-class sex work. Published: July 26, 2017.
  • Index.hu – Minute-by-minute live reporting, and political analysis of the James Comey hearing.
  • Mérce.hu – “What’s at stake at the Hong Kong protests?” and other freelance work.
  • Personal writing – excerpt from my Taipei Journals blog.
  • Print: World of Young Cinema (English), Műértő Magazin, Vince Magazin (Hungarian).

Webdesign

During an uneventful summer vacation, I taught myself basic HTML, CSS and PHP from online tutorials. I got my hands on Photoshop CS3, and started designing my own personal site. I made friends on a discussion board of amateur web designers and graphic designers who were hosting and tutoring each other.

My personal site lived through 13 iterations. It taught me skills that I use every single day. Designing this current (admittedly imperfect) site from scratch was a fantastic experience – I can still open a notepad and start building something from nothing. I have my 12-year-old self to thank for that.
