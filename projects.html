Ella has over a decade of experience in strategic communication, with a focus on event production and outreach. She led the communication of international projects at Hangvető, specifically MOST – the Bridge for Balkan Music and UPBEAT – the European Showcase Platform for World Music. During this time, she produced the Balkan World Music Guide and Balkan Festival Guide, distributed in 5000 print copies in 12 European countries.

Ella worked at festivals such as Bankito, Budapest Ritmo, PONT, Trilla, and many others. She was essential in the 2021 Budapest Ritmo rebranding, which resulted in record ticket sales, and facilitated professional training programs in the music industry such as the 2020 and 2021 CEU Music in Heritage summer university courses. Ella produced an award-nominated podcast series, Dalkímia, focusing on the creative process of songwriting.

RSD 12 Event Production Manager

I was the event production manager of the RSD 12 Washington, D.C. in-person conference, as well as the 15-day RSD 12 ONLINE event, streaming from 12 global locations. I worked closely with RSD 12’s chief organizer, Cheryl May, and the Georgetown RSD 12 team led by Dr. Evan Barba.

As the online event producer I successfully managed the access of over 500 participants and speakers total to Georgetown’s Zoom Events platform. I also made sure each session started on time, was recorded, and that technical help and troubleshooting with presentations was available to speakers.

As the in-person event producer, I coordinated a team of over 20 student volunteers to cover sessions, take photos, and manage front desk duties. I helped design and set-up the live stream from Georgetown’s on-site conference, and managed the live stream myself for the time of the event.

Culture Collective

Facilitated the launching process of a new student project at Georgetown’s Communication, Culture, & Technology department. I provided guidance on outreach, event organization, and production, and helped develop the core concepts that the club was built around. The Culture Collective was conceived of and designed by Paola García Acuña, and debuted with great success with guest speaker Axel Arth from the Milken Center for Advancing the American Dream.

Car Barn Academy

Relaunched a pre-pandemic series of events, Car Barn Academy at Georgetown’s Communication, Culture, & Technology department. CBA empowers students to share their marketable skills with each other, fostering invaluable skills like public speaking, leadership, and peer-to-peer education. I developed a Workshop Blueprint document to support the students' planning process. Currently working with multiple students to bring their workshops to life.

Balkan World Music Guide

The Balkan World Music Guide is a catalogue of the best that the Balkan has to offer in world and folk music. I pitched the concept, wrote and edited the content, and worked closely with the graphic designer, Nur Mohammed, to come up with the publication’s visual identity. I also coordinated the printing and distribution process of 5000 copies, shipping to 12 European countries in 2022. The Balkan World Music Guide has since become a staple publication for the MOST Music project and got republished every project year.

Bankito Festival, Photography Coordinator

Returning to Bankito Festival’s communication team every year since 2017, I was head of photography and social content in 2022. I coordinated a team of 6 young photographers over 4 festival days, covering events from dusk to dawn. I also closely supported Zsuzsa Kover, head of communication in 2022, with each aspect of the festival’s communication strategy.

Budapest Ritmo Rebranding

Budapest Ritmo is a city festival that needed rebranding after 5 years. I was a key figure in the rebranding effort, working under Sara Venczel, head of communication at Hangveto. I managed the redesign, deployment, and testing of the festival’s new website. I also rewrote the website copy in English and Hungarian. I also participated in every step of the visual rebranding process, working closely with designer Nur Mohammed. Our rebranding campaign, complete with billboard ads, innovative eco-friendly merch, light installations, print publications, and digital products, was praised by Kreativ, Hungary’s leading advertising and marketing industry publication. The 2022 edition of Budapest Ritmo also saw record ticket sales, sold out each night of the 3-day festival.

Budapest Ritmo Films Livestream

During lockdown Budapest Ritmo produced four music films in collaboration with leading Hungarian directors, world music bands, and the project sponsor, Bartók Spring International Art Weeks. I designed the online debut live stream experience, coordinating with Bartók Spring, and managed the social media live stream for all four movies, four consecutive nights.

Ad content management system

As a junior marketing specialist at Dreamjo.bs, I manually managed over 60 social media advertising campaigns, which prompted me to develop my own automated posting system. The company’s strategy was to micro-target software developers with highly specific skillsets to deliver applicants to our job postings. Much of the responsibility of delivering on this relied on marketing. Working as part of a small team and without a content management system, I developed a spreadsheet that helped us automate part of the work, allowing us to save time and scale up faster.
